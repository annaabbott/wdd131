About the Artist

For as long as Mariah can remember, she has created art: sketchbooks full of drawings, 3-ring binders full of manga-spinoffs, and doodles on the back of every scrap of paper she could find. Art is still her passion. She paints to fill her need to create. She finds inspiration in Japanese manga, Utah's mountains, and the people she loves.
